The mathematics courses combine content from streams A and B, covering Linear Algebra & Matrices, Complex Numbers, Vector Analysis, Calculus, Partial Differentiation, and Differential Equations

这些课程结合理论与实践，为学生的工程学学习和实际应用奠定坚实基础。工程力学课程为学生提供了学习静力学与动力学的机会，分别关注物体平衡分析和运动规律的应用，奠定了工程学科的基础。对于基础电路课程，学生可以通过电路设计学习如何构建基本电子电路，或通过电路分析深入理解电流、电压等电路行为的规律。在机电一体课程中，设计导论带领学生了解机械和电子的设计原则，而系统集成课程则将机械、电气和控制系统整合为实际项目。这些课程通过理论与实践的结合，全面提升学生的知识储备和应用能力，为未来的学术和职业发展打下坚实基础。

The engineering mechanics courses include Statics and Dynamics, helping students understand the fundamental principles of equilibrium and motion. The basic circuits courses cover Circuit Design and Circuit Analysis, where students learn to design and analyze complex circuits through experiments and project-based learning. The mechatronics courses offer Introduction to Mechatronic Design and System Integration, teaching students how to integrate mechanical, electrical, and control systems into practical applications.

These courses combine theory and practice, laying a solid foundation for students' engineering studies and real-world applications.The engineering mechanics courses provide students with opportunities to study statics and dynamics, focusing respectively on equilibrium analysis and the application of motion principles, forming the foundation of engineering disciplines. In basic circuits courses, students can learn to construct basic electronic circuits through circuit design or gain a deeper understanding of current, voltage, and other circuit behaviors through circuit analysis. In mechatronics courses, the Introduction to Design course introduces students to the principles of mechanical and electronic design, while the System Integration course focuses on combining mechanical, electrical, and control systems into practical projects. These courses combine theory with practice, enhancing students’ knowledge and application skills, and laying a solid foundation for their future academic and career development.
